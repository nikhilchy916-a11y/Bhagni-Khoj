About Us

Rooted in tradition, driven by youth

BKS CLUB (Bhagni Khoj Sports Club) is a youth-led cultural and sports organization dedicated to preserving Nepali traditions while promoting sportsmanship, leadership, and community engagement. Our club organizes Deusi-Bhailo celebrations, football tournaments, cultural programs, social service initiatives, and youth development activities to create a stronger and more united community.

Our Mission

  • Preserve Nepali culture and traditions
  • Encourage youth participation in sports
  • Promote teamwork, leadership & discipline
  • Organize community service & awareness programs

Our Vision

To become one of Nepal's leading youth clubs — inspiring cultural preservation, athletic excellence, and community development for generations to come.

"Ek Samaj, Ek Team"
One Community, One Team
What We Do

Our Activities

From the streets of Bhagni Khoj to the football pitch — every program builds culture, character, and community.

Deusi-Bhailo Programs

Traditional Tihar celebrations that carry Nepali folk culture to every doorstep.

Football Tournaments

Community leagues that build fitness, discipline, and local sporting talent.

Cultural Festivals

Dance, music, and heritage showcases that keep tradition alive for youth.

Community Service

Clean-up drives, relief support, and neighbourhood improvement projects.

Youth Leadership

Mentorship and training programs that shape the club's next generation of leaders.

Sports Training

Structured coaching camps in football and athletics for all skill levels.

Social Awareness

Campaigns on health, education, and civic responsibility for the local community.

Charity Events

Fundraisers and donation drives supporting families in need.
